Our History

Breakthrough parish was founded in 2010 with a vision to build a vibrant community of faith. Over the years, we have grown into a diverse congregation committed to worship, service, and outreach.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To build a community of believers who live and share the love of Christ every day.

Our Mission

To teach, inspire, and support our congregation through worship, outreach, and service.
